VigilAI — Safety Intelligence Demo

VigilAI is a personal safety monitoring system that can detect trip deviations, unusual stops, and non-response events and notify trusted contacts. It is designed as a proactive safety layer and does not require continuous tracking.

How it works

  • User explicitly starts a trip and selects trusted contacts.
  • The system evaluates movement, route deviation, and “silence” (no movement / no heartbeat / no response).
  • If risk exceeds thresholds, it sends a CHECK (“Are you OK?”) before escalating.

Messaging

Messages are transactional safety notifications sent only after the user opts in by starting a trip. Recipients may opt out by replying STOP.
